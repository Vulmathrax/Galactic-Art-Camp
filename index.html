Galactic Art Camp

Welcome to the Galactic Art Camp! A place where young artists explore their creativity across the cosmos. Over the course of one magical week, campers will paint starscapes, build lunar sculptures, and imagine entire galaxies through collaborative storytelling and visual art. Join us for a journey of color, expression, and planetary fun!

What to Expect

  • Hands-on art projects
  • Intergalactic storytelling
  • Creative games and movement
  • Friendly space crew mentors
